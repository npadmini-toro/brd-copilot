Requirement for a private equity firm:
Portfolio Company Performance Monitoring
The firm needs a centralized system to track and report on the financial and operational health of all portfolio companies in real time. This includes consolidating KPIs (revenue growth, EBITDA margins, cash burn, debt covenants), generating standardized reporting across companies with different ERP systems, and producing quarterly investor reports for LPs (Limited Partners) — all with role-based access controls to ensure sensitive data is shared only with the right stakeholders.
